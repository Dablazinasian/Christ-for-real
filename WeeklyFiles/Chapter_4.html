Chapter 4

A passage that could be read alongside this chapter: Matthew 19:16-30

As you read the chapter, consider how surrendering to Christ’s lordship might look in your everyday life.

A couple of examples: - Decisions: Instead of deciding based on personal preferences, pray for God’s guidance, trusting His direction. - Priorities: Reflect on areas where you may hold back from surrendering. Choose one area this week to yield fully to God. - Dependence: Practice letting go of self-reliance by inviting God’s help in daily tasks, viewing them as opportunities for faith growth.
